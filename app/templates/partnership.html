The Vision

The Five Arms of Partnership

Five channels of Kingdom impact — choose one or more as the Lord leads you.

Scholarship & Education

Support children's education, scholarships, tuition assistance, school supplies and academic development.

Proverbs 22:6

How funds are used

Tuition support, school fees, books, uniforms, and academic mentoring for children who would otherwise miss classroom opportunities.

Ministries impacted

Scholarship desk, school outreach teams, and education advocacy within Women of Passion and Purpose.

Expected outcomes

More children remaining in school, improved literacy, and families strengthened through education.

Scripture: “Train up a child in the way he should go…” — Proverbs 22:6

Welfare

Support food distribution, clothing, shelter, healthcare and emergency assistance.

Proverbs 19:17

How funds are used

Food parcels, clothing drives, temporary shelter support, medical assistance, and crisis relief for vulnerable households.

Ministries impacted

Welfare outreach, hospital visitation, and community care teams.

Expected outcomes

Immediate relief for families in need and restored dignity through compassionate care.

Scripture: “Whoever is kind to the poor lends to the Lord…” — Proverbs 19:17

Mission & Ministry

Support evangelism, crusades, Bible distribution, ministry materials and global outreach.

Mark 16:15

How funds are used

Crusade logistics, Bibles and tracts, media outreach, travel for mission teams, and discipleship materials.

Ministries impacted

Evangelism, media ministry, and cross-border mission partnerships.

Expected outcomes

Souls reached with the Gospel and new believers grounded in the Word.

Scripture: “Go into all the world and preach the gospel…” — Mark 16:15

Empowerment

Support vocational training, business grants, skills acquisition and financial empowerment programmes.

John 10:10

How funds are used

Skills workshops, starter grants, tools for trades, and mentoring for micro-enterprise.

Ministries impacted

Empowerment programmes, vocational training hubs, and marketplace discipleship.

Expected outcomes

Sustainable livelihoods, reduced dependency, and families walking in abundance.

Scripture: “I have come that they may have life, and have it to the full.” — John 10:10

Children Ministry

Support Bible clubs, feeding programmes, children's outreach and Christian discipleship.

Matthew 19:14

How funds are used

Bible clubs, children's feeding, teaching materials, camps, and safe outreach spaces.

Ministries impacted

Children's church, holiday clubs, and family discipleship initiatives.

Expected outcomes

Children encountering Jesus early and growing in faith, character, and hope.

Scripture: “Let the little children come to me…” — Matthew 19:14

How To Become A Partner

A clear path from calling to confirmation.

  1. 1

    Choose one or more Partnership Arms

    Select Scholarship & Education, Welfare, Mission & Ministry, Empowerment, and/or Children Ministry.

  2. 2

    Complete the Partnership Registration Form

    Share your details and partnership amount using the secure form below.

  3. 3

    Choose your Commitment Schedule

  4. 4

    Select your Preferred Payment Method

  5. 5

    Receive Partnership Confirmation

    We will confirm your partnership and keep you connected to the impact of your giving.
