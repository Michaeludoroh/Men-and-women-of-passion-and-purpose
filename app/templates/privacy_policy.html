Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information.

Last Updated: July 13, 2026

1. Introduction

Men and Women of Passion and Purpose (“MWPP,” “WOPP,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) operates the website at https://woppandmopp.com and the WOPP mobile application (the “App”). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using our website or App, you agree to the practices described here.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect information you provide directly and information collected automatically when you use our services.

  • Information you submit through forms, account registration, membership, prayer requests, or support messages
  • Technical and usage data needed to operate, secure, and improve our website and App
  • Payment-related status information from Apple or Google when you purchase or manage a subscription (we do not store full card numbers)

3. Account Information

When you create an account in the App or on related services, we may collect your name, profile details, authentication credentials, and account preferences. This information is used to identify you, personalize your experience, and provide access to membership features.

4. Email Address

We collect your email address when you register, subscribe, contact support, submit forms, or request updates. We use email to send account notices, membership confirmations, ministry communications you opt into, and responses to your inquiries. You may unsubscribe from non-essential marketing emails where applicable.

5. Subscription Information

If you enroll in a membership or paid subscription, we may process subscription status, plan type, renewal dates, and related account entitlements. Actual payment processing for in-app purchases is handled by Apple or Google, not by MWPP directly.

6. Push Notifications

With your permission, the App may use device push notification tokens to send ministry updates, event reminders, teaching alerts, and other service notices. You can disable notifications at any time in your device settings. Disabling notifications does not delete your account.

7. Firebase Usage

We may use Google Firebase services (such as Cloud Messaging, Analytics, Crashlytics, or Authentication-related tools) to deliver notifications, monitor App stability, and improve performance. Firebase may process device and usage data according to Google’s privacy practices. For more information, review Firebase Privacy and Security.

8. Payment Processing

Apple In-App Purchase

Subscriptions and digital purchases made on iOS are processed by Apple through In-App Purchase / App Store billing. Apple handles payment credentials under Apple’s privacy policy. MWPP receives purchase confirmation and entitlement status as needed to unlock features.

Google Play Billing

Subscriptions and digital purchases made on Android are processed by Google through Google Play Billing. Google handles payment credentials under Google’s privacy policy. MWPP receives purchase confirmation and entitlement status as needed to unlock features.

For billing disputes, refunds, or payment method changes, contact Apple or Google (as applicable) through your store account. For membership access issues after a successful purchase, contact support@woppandmopp.com.

9. Cookies (Website Only)

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies for essential functionality, security, session management, and (where enabled) analytics or preference storage. You can control cookies through your browser settings. Blocking certain cookies may affect website features. The mobile App does not rely on website cookies in the same way.

10. Data Retention

We retain personal information only as long as needed to provide our services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, enforce agreements, and maintain ministry/business records. When information is no longer required, we delete or anonymize it in accordance with our retention practices, unless a longer period is required by law.

11. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards designed to protect personal information against unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or alteration. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

12. Children’s Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact support@woppandmopp.com and we will take appropriate steps to delete it.

13. Third-Party Services

We may share or process information with trusted third parties that help us operate our services, including:

  • Apple App Store / In-App Purchase
  • Google Play Billing
  • Google Firebase
  • Hosting, email, analytics, and security providers
  • Payment processors used for website giving or partnership (where applicable)

These providers process data only as needed to perform services for us or as required by their own policies and law. Links to third-party sites or platforms are governed by those parties’ privacy policies.

14. Contact Information

For privacy questions, data requests, or concerns about this policy, contact us:

  • Email: support@woppandmopp.com
  • Website: https://woppandmopp.com
  • Support page: Support

15. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last Updated” date at the top of this page reflects the latest revision. Continued use of our website or App after changes means you accept the updated policy.
